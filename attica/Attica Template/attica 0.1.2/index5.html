about us

From Travellers for Travellers 

As an Avid Traveller I wanted to Creat a Home That welcomes People from all over the World

Zeno Discovered Iceland in 2016 and Has settled ever Since

For over 9 years I am lucky to welcome guest from all over the globe as our living room worldmap shows. (Over 9 years we have welcomed guest from over 120 coutnries) 


On my arrival in 2006 I fell in love with the peninsula where my houses are located. This tranquil neighbourhood is only 10 minutes away from downtown but offers plenty of incredible sights. Esja Mountain, Faxafloi Bay, Snaefellsjökull glacier, Valhúsa Park, Grótta Lights and the all so popular northern lights are things to be seen here.

In the humble beginnings in 2009 I renovated the House myself and became a popular travellers spot right away. New we offer on 3 locations a wide selection from economy Rooms to Family Apartments, from cozy to to incredible Seaview at our yellow House.


I hope that my team and me can welcome you soon and help you on your travels in Iceland. Drop us a line if you have questions or use our AI-Chatbot before arrival and remember the cheapest price guaranteed is directly through our website.
